Carolina Torreblanca

¡Hola! I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Politics at New York University.

In Fall 2024, I will join the Penn Development Research Initiative as a postdoctoral fellow.

My research explores the political economy of crime and its punishment, with a regional focus in Mexico and Latin America, and an emphasis on political methodology.

I hold a B.A. in Political Science and a B.A. in International Relations from ITAM (2015). Before coming to NYU, I was the Director of Data Analysis in Data Cívica.

If you want to read me rant about Mexican politics and occasionally movies, you can follow me on Twitter. I am also working on an ever-expanding compilation of the best cover songs you can check out.
